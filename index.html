SYNAPS-T
Interactive Game Table

date :

12/2016

softwares :

autors :

Alexis Huet & Thibaut Lacroix

Nowadays, it is becoming more and more difficult to dispense with his phone. With friends, in the restaurant or around a drink, who never pulled out his smartphone to pass, in the end, more time with eyes on the device than in those of the person opposite? This project is based on the creation of an interactive game table with the aim of putting the screen at the heart of our social relations. Thanks to the theme of the game present since our childhood, this connected table aims to get the user of his smartphone for a moment of sharing and thus privilege social relations, communication and collective play.
